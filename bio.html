GN JAYA CLARK

Jaya Clark – Dedicated WASH and Humanitarian Professional


BIO/CERTS

Jaya Clark is a highly skilled and results-driven WASH Officer with four years of progressive experience in the Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) sector.

Born in Kabwir LGA of Plateau State, Nigeria, Clark was brought up in sound mind and in the Christian way, strive through the stress and hardship of Nigerian economy to become who he is today.

With a background in Chemistry and multiple certifications in WASH-related disciplines—including

  • Community-Led Total Sanitation (CLTS) for Professionals,
  • WASH in Emergencies
  • Global WASH Assessment Cluster
  • Prevention of Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (PSEA) with UNICEF
  • Introduction to WASH
  • Household Water Treatment and Storage System (HWTSS)

Clark has demonstrated exceptional technical and leadership abilities in improving public health outcomes in underserved communities.
